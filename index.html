We Run The Nodes

If a coin stakes, we will pool all assets together. So new deposits from members will go into the staking pool until there is enough to build a masternode. That way, new members get to share in masternode and staking rewards with members who are in active masternodes. All assets are generating dividends and everyone wins. If a coin doesn't stake, then only members in active masternodes will receive dividends until the new deposits have reached the necessary collateral for a masternode.

Run the Nodes will build all new nodes and pay out on the day specified in the coin channel. Everyone will receive a payout and if they wish to reinvest, the member will be required to submit an additional transaction to reinvest. Run the Nodes will take 4% of all new rewards to help to pay my team and business expenses. Business expenses include VPS services, website maintenance, etc.
